SubExtractor is a Chrome extension developed by TapShiftDev that helps users extract and view captions from online videos. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our extension.

üîí Data Collection

SubExtractor operates entirely within your browser:

  • Automatically cleans up stored data to prevent memory issues
