Privacy Policy

On-device processing. No tracking. No data collection.

TL;DR: We don’t collect any personal data. SubExtractor works entirely on your device.

Overview

SubExtractor is a Chrome & Safari (iOS & macOS) extension by TapShiftDev that helps you extract and view captions. This policy explains how your information is handled.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Video content
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device fingerprints

We process locally:

  • Caption text
  • Temporary cache
  • User preferences

How It Works

  • Captions are accessed directly from supported platforms
  • Processing happens only on your device
  • Temporary storage may be used for performance
  • Data is never transmitted to servers

Third-Party Services

SubExtractor only retrieves caption files or minimal metadata from video platforms. These are governed by each platform’s privacy policy.

Data Storage

  • All data remains local to your device
  • Old data is automatically cleared
  • No cloud storage
  • Uninstalling clears all data

Security

  • Strict content security policy
  • Minimal permissions only
  • No external scripts
  • Regular updates

User Rights

No personal data means no tracking or profiling. You maintain full control locally and can uninstall at any time.

Updates

This policy may change. Any updates will appear here. Continued use indicates acceptance.
